# Ethics and Law for Data Science

## 📌 Overview
This folder documents my coursework and reflections from AASD4003 — Ethics and Law for Data Science.

It is not just theory. It is about developing the judgment to build AI systems that are fair, legal, and responsible — and being able to defend those decisions to a client, a regulator, or a courtroom.

It is a live, honest record. You will see evolving thinking, case study notes, and ideas still being formed. That is intentional.

## My Learning Philosophy
Data Science without ethics is just optimization without conscience.

Understanding the law is not a constraint on innovation — it is the foundation that makes innovation trustworthy.

Learning in public. Building with integrity.
